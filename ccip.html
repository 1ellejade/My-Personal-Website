Consulting Cup Integrated Project

Sophomore year competition for Gabelli Business Students

The Consulting Cup integrated project was my most memorable and valuable experience at the Gabelli School. The project was an opportunity to bring together learnings from all of my foundational business classes, from Accounting and Information Systems to Management and Marketing. My team and I had to meet tight deadlines for deliverables in each of these areas in addition to developing our project presentation, all the while learning to efficiently delegate work and manage team conflict.

One of our most rigorous courses of the semester, despite only meeting once a week, was the Business Communications course. We were required to present each class, at first by ourselves and then with a group. While on the first day I was so nervous that I could barely get through my 30-second presentation, by the end of the semester I was confident and prepared. This project has helped me to develop lifelong communication skills that I have leveraged in every subsequent presentation.

This course also taught the importance of effective visual communication, for which my team was awarded the Outstanding Visuals Award in addition to our award of 4th place. Feel free to watch our full presentation here.
